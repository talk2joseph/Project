Welcome to PALMVILLE COLLEGE

The college, we have a well-trained and dedicated staff with a friendly disposition. We have well-equipped laboratories manned by skilled professionals, well-ventilated classrooms that seat no more than 25 students per class, a state-of-the-art information technology laboratory. And a technical drawing studio. Our students have painted on the walls of our cultural and creative art studio, which is well equipped with donkeys and beautified with art works by our students. We train students not just to be academically sound so they can pass their examinations, but we also try to inculcate the right morals too. In addition, we have a very spacious field with natural grass for our sporting activities. We also have two large halls that can seat more than 200 people for any kind of indoor events or examinations. Most of our students get admitted into the choicest universities on leaving school the same year. We also offer a flexible payment plan for school fees to our esteemed parents and guardians. Our doors are open for verification of the above claims and you will be glad to bring your children and wards to Palmville College.

We teach Children through words and example the values of dedication, commitment, integrity, hard and smart work.

We understand the delicate starting phase of children, and make sure we handle them with the utmost level of care and attention.
